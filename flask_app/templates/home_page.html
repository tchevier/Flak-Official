Who we are:

Flak is a three piece band full of lifelong friends from Centennial Colorado. Our love for music sparked a flame into the passion and drive we have for Rock n' Roll. Creating fast, edgy, and powerful but passionate riffs, we bring a sound and energy that fills ourselves with an animalistic sense of euphoria.

Mission:

Our mission is to help bring bands back better than ever. We strive to build a community of friends and family around, to share these amazing experiences with.
